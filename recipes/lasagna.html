Lasagna


Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait.
You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below,
but let's go over the basics:



Ingredients

  • 600g Meat
  • 1 clove of gralic
  • 1 onion
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tsp of sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 400g lasagna noodles
  • 300g cheese of choice
  • 1 egg


How to Make Lasagna Step-By-Step

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Grind the cheese
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving


How to layer lasgna

  1. Meat sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Cheese
  4. Meat sauce
  5. Cheese
  6. Repeat the layers, then top with the remaining cheese


How Long to Cook Lasagna

The assembled lasagna should take about 50 minutes to cook in an oven preheated to 375 degrees F. Cover it with foil for the first 25 minutes, then let it cook uncovered for the final 25 minutes. Also, it's important to let the lasagna rest at room temperature for about 15 minutes before you cut into it.
